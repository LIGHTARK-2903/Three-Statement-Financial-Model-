## ðŸ§  Model Structure Overview

### **1. Assumptions Sheet**
Contains all editable drivers:
- Revenue growth  
- Operating margins  
- Capex & Depreciation  
- Working capital days  
- Tax rate  
- Interest & financing assumptions  

### **2. Income Statement**
- Revenue build-up  
- EBITDA, EBIT  
- PBT, Net Income  
- Margin calculations  

### **3. Balance Sheet**
- Assets (PPE, Current Assets, Cash)  
- Liabilities (Borrowings, Payables)  
- Equity and retained earnings linkage  

### **4. Cash Flow Statement**
- Operating cash flow  
- Investing cash flow (Capex)  
- Financing cash flow (Debt movements)  
- Automatic closing cash calculation  

All three statements are **linked**, ensuring internal consistency.
